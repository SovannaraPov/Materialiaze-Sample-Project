 Digital Marketing
-> Summary of Position

 EASYBOOK (CAMBODIA) CO., LTD is looking for Digital Marketing Executive. As a Digital Marketing Executive, you'll be working with regional marketing team and be part of the digital marketing team to design, build and maintain online presence for company internet properties. We are looking for candidate with high interest in digital marketing career growth, you will have opportunity to work in overall marketing channel including but not limited in search engine optimization (SEO), online advertising (Google Adwords, Facebook Ads, Native Ads, Media Buying), email marketing, social media marketing, affiliate marketing and etc. Execute SEO strategy for the company local websites and related internet properties.


-> Main duties and responsibitlities
  1. Monitor, track and report on SEO performance and status
  2. Effectively implement, manage & optimize ROI on Advertising Campaigns (AdWords, Facebook, Native Ads, Display Ads & etc)
  3. Identify, innovate on and experiment with new ad types, angles and alternate channels
  4. Data collection & analyse existing campaigns activities & build reports for marketing campaigns
  5. Work with internal team (Marketing, Programming, IT Support, Sales) to carry out the digital marketing tasks
  6. Good translation on our website asap to khmer Lang

-> Other Requirement
  • > 2~3 years working experience in digital / e-commerce environment & passion digital marketing fields
  • > Strong analytical skills (think: Google Analytics, Excel) and data driven thinking (think: acquisition funnels, AB Testing)
  • > Self-motivated with the ability to work independently and as a team player
